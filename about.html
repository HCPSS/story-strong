Everyone has a story

About the Project

Student Voices is the product of HCPSS school leaders and curriculum writers who were looking for a new, innovative way for students to have their voices heard. The overall goal is to provide a place for stories to exist that are unique to HCPSS students, giving other students, community members, and the larger HCPSS community insight into our diverse student population.

Submitted stories are showcased on this site in a variety of different formats, and is encouraged to be shared on social media and student blogs. All content is created by students, prompted by questions to create a conversation about a particular topic. The current question is highlighted on the homepage of the student voices site, and all previous questions are archived for future viewers.

This project is the first of it’s kind at HCPSS, and we hope it will continue for many years to come.

Who can participate?

Students currently enrolled at a Howard County High or Middle School can submit stories to studentvoices@hcpss.org. The topic for this round of student voices is “What’s Shaped You?”. Answers to this question can be submitted in a variety of formats.
